Choose a race. This choice will determine the stat array that you will be using and what traits one can use.

Contact a staff member to roll for a Bloodline. This will give you a trait. Traits given by bloodline might be unable to be evolved depending on the trait.

Choose traits. You may start with:
1 Rare
2 uncommon
or 3 common traits.
Alternate step 3.
You may instead of choosing traits as normal choose to gamble for a high rarity trait. You will then gain 1 trait of the rarity you win of your choice as long as it still has slots. Create a ticket, and a staff member will spin the wheel for you. Select one of the two spun traits. Staff will also spin for family.

Spend your starting stats and keep track of any bonuses you may have. % bonuses are additive so if you have 20% buff and a 30% buff you have a 50% buff.

Once you have completed your character sheet, submit the sheet in the character submissions channel and wait to be approved by a member of staff.
